DISCLAIMER: These things aren't ordered from least to greatest, just 5 things I like from each type of media.

Fictional Characters

#1: Monkey D. Luffy
Media: One Piece

The reason Luffy is one my top 5 favorite characters is because he's funny, goofy, and caring. throughout the show he's making new friends by just being himself. If his friends are in trouble he makes sure to help them.
Most times even when he meets someone new that he barely just met, he will do anything to help them.

#2: Ruby Rose
Media: RWBY

The reason Ruby is one of my top 5 favorite characters is because she represents the idea that you don't need to be perfect. Throughout the show she makes a lot of mistakes, and those mistakes feel even more heavy because she's a team leader. She shows it's okay to fail, and you don't have to keep a brave face all the time. And just like Luffy, she can be really goofy at times, clumsy or just silly.

#3: Eikichi Onizuka
Media: Great Teacher Onizuka

The reason Onizuka is one of my top 5 favorite characters is because in the show he goes through life without worrying too much about anything. He's not really a serious character, and acts really immature for his age. But he shows that sometimes in life you just have to relax a bit, make little things count. He teaches you how to live your life. One of my favorite quotes from him is"It doesn't matter what some people say. Right? As long as you're sure about how you really feel. You focus on that and everything else just disappears."

#4: Son Goku
Media: Dragon Ball

The Reason Goku is one of my top 5 favorite characters is because of his determination to get stronger. According to the story he was actually considered a weak Saiyan, but ever since he was a kid, he's constanstly getting stronger than people who were once stronger than him. Goku proves that if you work hard enough and have a strong focus you can achieve your goals.

#5: Kamina
Media: Gurren Lagann

The Reason Kamina is one of my top 5 favorite characters he shows how sometimes to be the best you, you just need a little push, or a BIG push from someone. He kept pushing his brother to break out of his shell and become stronger. One of his quotes is literally "Kick logic to the curb and do the impossible!" Basically saying stop caring if the goal seems impossbile, go for it.
